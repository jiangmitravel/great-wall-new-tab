## Extension Name
Great Wall of China Travel - New Tab Theme

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Stunning Great Wall of China photography for your new tab. Features 10 high-quality images across different seasons and times.

## Detailed Description

**Experience the Majesty of the Great Wall**

Transform your Chrome new tab with breathtaking photography of the Great Wall of China. Each time you open a new tab, you'll see a different stunning view of this ancient wonder.

**Features:**
- 10 high-quality images showcasing the Great Wall across different seasons
- Random background selection for variety
- Clean, minimal interface with time display
- Integrated Google search
- Fast and lightweight
- No data collection or tracking

**What's Included:**
- Sunset and sunrise views
- Autumn foliage
- Winter snow scenes
- Spring morning mist
- Summer landscapes
- Twilight and starry skies

**Privacy:**
- No permissions required
- No data collection
- No external connections
- Works completely offline

**Inspired to Visit?**

If these beautiful images make you want to experience the Great Wall in person, you can find helpful travel guides and tips at greatwalltravelguide.com, including:
- How to get from Beijing to the Great Wall
- Which section to visit (Badaling, Mutianyu, etc.)
- Historical background and interesting facts
- Photography tips and best visiting times

Perfect for lovers of travel, history, architecture, and world heritage sites.
